This is Trey.

Hover


My name is Treyvon and I’m from Astoria, Queens. I love listening to and making music.
It’s really a passion and I’ve been doing it for quite some time, since high school actually, maybe before.
You have to enjoy doing this kind of thing because it is very time consuming.
Sometimes you can make beats in half an hour but sometimes it can take days.
Whether you’re finding inspiration or can’t get motivated, whatever it may be.
It varies from time to time and it’s okay. I go with how I’m feeling and
that helps me make the connection to my listeners. I can be in a good mood and
make something fun and random or if I’m in a slump I can express that in my music.
I take my laptop with me on the go and whenever I get any ideas or inspiration
I am able to make cook something up right there or record it on my phone.
I want to make it big someday.
I have some of my works on the next page if you’d like to give it a listen,
if you’d like to support me follow me on my social media and spread the word!!
If you’re interested in business please go to contact me.
